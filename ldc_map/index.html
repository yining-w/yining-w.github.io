Understand the Map

Each country is classified into one of four groups based on their participation in international or regional learning assessments (ILSA/RLSA).

Group 1 countries do not have any ILSA/RLSAs in the last 7 years. Group 2 countries have one ILSA/RLSA in the last 5-7 years. Group 3 are countries with two or more in the last 5-7 years. Group 4 represents countries that have two or more ILSA/RLSA in the last 3 years.
